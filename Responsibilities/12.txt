As an Internal Audit Data Analyst with Frost, you will be responsible for supporting internal auditors with quantitative and qualitative analysis of financial, transactional, and behavioral data. Responsibilities will include understanding the operations, internal controls, laws and regulations, Board level policies and interrelationships of the audited areas; planning analytical work in accordance with audit objectives; adhering to data analysis standards and industry best practices; supervising audits or special projects as assigned by the Audit Project Manager, as audit skills and adherence to standards develop; and maintaining on-site control over all reports, records, and data involved in the analytics process until the audit is completed. The candidate will present completed work papers and detailed reports of audit findings and recommendations, highlighting irregularities or exceptions to Audit Project Leader in a timely fashion and demonstrating increasing levels of quality as to style and content.
